SITUATION OVERVIEW

Rogue SSC sub-faction "CARMINE FALLOUT" has seized the Hellsir Arcology’s defensive nexus. The platform's four Leviathan HAC auto-turrets have been subverted with hostile IFF codes, currently targeting any Union-aligned signature. Enemy forces are using the turrets as force multipliers to lock down the sector. Your fireteam must capture and hold each turret control node long enough to revert targeting parameters and turn the defensive network against CARMINE FALLOUT.

Intel confirms the presence of a Goliath-Class DAGUN Frame leading the assault, supported by mercenary infantry. Hold the line, and regain control of the turrets before time runs out.

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE: CONTROL ZONES

CZ ALPHA • BRAVO • CHARLIE • DELTA Four strategic hardpoints outfitted with . Control as many as possible at the end of each round to build your score. Contest zones by having more units within the CZ radius than the enemy, or clear opposing forces.

CONTROL MISSION PROTOCOL

SCORING: At the end of each round, each side gains 1 point for each Control Zone (CZ) they control. If one side controls all four CZs at the end of a round, they gain an additional +1 point (total 5 points that round).

VICTORY CONDITIONS

🏆 PC Victory: The PCs have the highest score at the end of the sixth round.
💀 Enemy Victory: If 3 or more turret nodes are permanently destroyed or CARMINE FALLOUT have the highest score at the end of the sixth round.
⚖️ Stalemate: If both sides have an equal score at the end of the sixth round, there is no victor and both sides must withdraw.

CONTROLLING A ZONE: A side controls a CZ if they have at least one unit within the zone's radius and the opposing side has zero units inside that same zone. If both sides have units present, the zone is contested and neither side scores it.

SECONDARY OBJECTIVES

  • Confirm Reports of Artillery Mech: If true, an enemy artillery frame greatly threatens friendly units. Confirm these reports and eliminate the threat if any.
  • Disable Goliath DAGUN Frame: Heavy assault unit will attempt to break your defensive line. Bring it down for bonus rewards and to deny enemy zone-breaking power.
